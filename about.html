About Me

I am a third year engineering student currently studying at Queen's University. One thing I will never be thankful for enough, is having a genuine passion and love for the things I do. Often times, school does not feel like work, rather challenges that drive me to push the boundaries of what I once believed I was only capable of. Being able to apply the things I've learned in to practicality brings me joy and accomplishment.

I enjoy creating my own models and 3d printing them in my free time. I also like to code, which is suprisingly very prevelant in the majority of mechanical courses here at my school. Coding my own website in html isn't the most common conduct for non-developers, but then again, I really do enjoy a good challenge. Some of my other hobbies include playing volleyball, hitting the gym, keeping a tab on F1 and the NBA, as well as skiing in the winter months.

For my future career, I hope it aligns with my interests just like school does, and doesn't feel like work. I hope it takes me to places I have never been before, literally an figuratively. I hope it gives me the oppertunity to meet more like-minded people that I am able to connect with inside and outside of the workplace. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit more about me.
