About Me

I am a third year engineering student currently studying at Queen's University. One thing I will never be thankful for enough, is having genuine passion and love for the things I do. Being able to apply what I've learned in to practicality brings me joy and accomplishment. I always do what I can to bring positivive value from a technical and personal approach. Being apart of something bigger than myself often brings the best out of me. As much indviduality is always appreciated, I believe productivity and efficency comes from a well oiled collective.

I enjoy creating my own models and 3d printing them in my free time. I also like to code, which is suprisingly very prevelant in the majority of mechanical courses here at my school. Some of my other hobbies include playing volleyball, skiing in the winter months, hitting the gym, as well as keeping a tab on F1 and the NBA.

For my future career, I aspire to find a path that aligns with my passions and feels fulfilling rather than like work. I hope it offers opportunities to explore uncharted territories, taking me to places I have never been before, both literally and figuratively. I look forward to meeting like-minded individuals with whom I can build meaningful connections, inside and outside of the proffessional enviorment. Thank you for taking the time to learn about me.
