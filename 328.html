Linkage Sim

As part of the final project of my dynamics course, student were tasked with designing a jumping mechanism with lingae members and muscles which apply a constant force. Using vector derivatives and Newtonian law, the project require for the derivation of the equations of motions of our design. I received inspiration from my proffessor when he mentioned that a completed set of equations can model a system in it's entierty. I spent the next 2 days exploring MATLAB's powerful graphing capabilities in hopes of bringing visuals to my design. Making use of the symbolic toolbox and creating my own animation function, I was able to create a visual representation of my final project.
